## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of `django-tenant-authx` seriously.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please **DO NOT** open an issue.
Instead, please email **rajidkk34@gmail.com** directly.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeframe for a fix.
